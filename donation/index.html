What support pays for

APGO will make funding disclosures more detailed as the organization grows. These are the baseline commitments.

01

Reporting & research

Primary documents, data access, interviews, travel when justified and the time to verify claims properly.

02

Products & infrastructure

Hosting, engineering and design for the Journal, Football Index, Dictionary and future public tools.

03

Independence safeguards

Support is not a transaction for editorial outcomes. APGO retains the right—and obligation—to reach unwelcome conclusions.
