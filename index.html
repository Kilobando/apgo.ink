The first stage of planning for a future team began.

April 9th, 2023

Initial ideas about geopolitics were discussed.

August 23rd, 2023

The first version of the team was established.

Plans for a future chat application program were introduced.

The organization’s interest in journalism was sparked.

Core Values

Morality

Adherence to a strict moral code, ensuring all actions are ethically sound.

Transparency

Openness and honesty in communication, fostering trust and accountability.

Equality

Commitment to treating everyone with fairness and justice, regardless of their background.

Fraternity

Promoting a spirit of brotherhood and solidarity among all members of society.

Teamwork

Collaboration and unity are at the core of our success, ensuring collective progress.

Innovation

Continuous pursuit of new ideas and technologies to stay ahead in a dynamic world.

Accuracy

Ensuring that all information shared is precise and reliable, maintaining integrity.

Compassion

Understanding and addressing the needs and concerns of those we serve.
