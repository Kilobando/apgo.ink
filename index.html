Working rules

Build trust into the format.

APGO’s projects can look different. Their underlying obligations should stay the same.

01

Label the kind of claim

News, analysis, opinion, methodology and experiment are not interchangeable. Readers should know which one they are entering.

02

Show the original material

Whenever possible, facts link to the document, dataset or text that supports them—not a chain of recycled summaries.

03

Revise in public

Corrections and model changes remain visible. A growing project earns authority by improving without rewriting its own history.

Reader funded

Help make the next thing real.

Support funds reporting, data access, design and infrastructure. It never purchases editorial influence or a preferred ranking.
