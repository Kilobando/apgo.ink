Founding and Development Timeline

2024

January 22nd, 2023

Populi Advocātiō was founded as a Christian organization by Kilo and Alpha.

Falcon joined and became a co-founder.

March 28th, 2023

The first stage of planning for a future team began.

April 9th, 2023

Initial ideas about geopolitics were discussed.

August 23rd, 2023

The first version of the team was established.

September 9th, 2023

Plans for a future chat application program were introduced.

September 24th, 2023

The organization’s interest in journalism was sparked.

October 1st, 2023

Completed key studies on history and geopolitics; new team members joined.

October 6th, 2023

Development of the chat application began.

November 28th, 2023

The chat project was postponed; ideas about a future company were conceptualized.

January 5th, 2024

The organization’s interest in journalism deepened, with private articles written for internal evaluation.

March 6th, 2024

Saba was recruited into the team.

March 10th, 2024

The idea of establishing a new company was discussed.

March 24th, 2024

Continued work on the chat application and website.

May 9th, 2024

Began writing private works and documentaries to train and educate the team.

August 7th, 2024

Crypto Cipher Studios was established legally, and a partnership was formed with Populi Advocātiō.

Core Values

Morality

Adherence to a strict moral code in all actions and decisions.

Loyalty

Commitment to the organization and the people it serves.

Commitment

Dedication to the mission and relentless pursuit of truth.

Transparency

Openness and honesty in communication, ensuring clarity in sources and intentions.

Integrity

Maintaining ethical standards and integrity in all dealings.

Innovation

Continuously striving for innovation and adapting to changing dynamics.

Courage

Boldly confronting challenges and speaking truth to power without fear.

Unity

Promoting unity and collaboration within the organization and the broader community.
