Our standard

Independent by design

Professionalism is not a tone of voice. It is a set of visible habits that readers can test.

01

Evidence beside argument

Reported facts link to primary documents whenever possible. Analysis is clearly labelled, and uncertainty stays in the sentence.

02

Methods readers can inspect

Rankings, scores and visualizations publish their rules, data windows and revisions instead of hiding judgment behind a number.

03

Funding without editorial control

Reader support funds the work. Donations do not purchase coverage, removal, access to drafts or favorable conclusions.
